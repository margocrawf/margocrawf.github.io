Olin Aquatic Robotic Systems

Olin Aquatic Robotic Systems (formerly Olin Robotic Sailing) is a project team dedicated to making autonomous boats. Last year, I worked with the mechanical team to machine parts for the rudder and lining systems, did composite layups, and designed mechanisms for the rigging using constant force springs and a linear actuator.

This year, I am working with the Software subteam to create programs for our new boat, primarily in Python, using ROS.
